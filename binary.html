Binary Converter
Computers, unlike us, humans, can only understand 0s and 1s which is called a Binary System(Base-2 Numeral system). This converter lets you convert strings, numbers and special characters into binary numbers.
Example - the letter 'A' to '1000001', the numeric number '1' to '110001'.
Usage
To convert a letter, a sentence, a number or a special character into binary digits, enter what you want to convert in the input text box and press 'Convert'.
